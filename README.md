# Global-Energy-Consumption-Renewable-Generation-Analysis-Power-BI

This project was conceived with a clear objective: to delve into industry data and harness its 
potential for comprehensive analysis. The ultimate goal is to craft a Business Intelligence (BI) 
solution that delivers an interactive dashboard tailored for strategy consultants. This solution, 
intended as a final product for clients, is designed to empower them in scrutinizing their data 
on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis. The overarching aim is to revolutionize traditional data 
analysis tools like Excel, power point models. 

Top Consuming Continents & Countries: Unveiling the continents and countries that lead in 
energy consumption.

Bottom Consuming Continents & Countries: Identifying the continents and countries with the 
lowest energy consumption. 

Top Producing Countries & Continents: Spotlighting the leading countries and continents in 
energy production. 

Highest Renewable Energy Production by Countries & Continents: Showcasing the countries 
and continents at the forefront of renewable energy production. 

Total Energy Produced & Total Consumed: Providing an overview of the overall energy 
production and consumption. 

Total Renewable & Non-Renewable Produced: Distinguishing between the production of 
renewable and non-renewable energy. 

Different Types of Production in Renewable & Non-Renewable: Exploring the various 
production types within renewable and non-renewable energy sources. 

Consumption in Each Type: Analyzing the consumption patterns across different energy 
production types. 


Key Influencing Factors in Increase/Decrease of Production & Consumption: Unraveling the 
factors influencing fluctuations in both production and consumption. 

Analyzing Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) with a specified target involves assessing the 
deviation between the current value and its target. Significance is determined by comparing 
this variance to other segments, whether it is high or low. In contrast, KPI analysis without a 
target focuses on the absolute value, identifying those that are either high or low relative to 
other segments. (aphilip94, 2023) 

Year Wise Breakdown of Consumption & Production: Providing a chronological breakdown 
of energy consumption and production over the years. 

Minimum, Average, and Highest Consumption Throughout the Years: Highlighting the range 
of energy consumption, including minimum, average, and maximum values. 

Percentage of Change in Consumption Observed for Countries/Continents: Quantifying the 
percentage change in consumption for countries and continents. 

Each Country’s Contribution on Renewable & Non-Renewable Energy Generation: 
Illustrating individual countries' contributions to both renewable and non-renewable energy 
generation. 

Contribution by Each Type of Energy Generation: Evaluating the share of each energy 
generation type in the overall energy landscape. 

Key Influencing Factors on Each Type of Generation Mode: Delving into the factors 
influencing different modes of energy generation.
